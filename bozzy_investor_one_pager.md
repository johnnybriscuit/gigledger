# Bozzy Investor One-Page Summary

## What Bozzy Is

Bozzy is a business operations platform for self-employed musicians and other independent freelancers. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, invoicing tools, mileage logs, receipt folders, and tax-prep handoff work with a single workflow for tracking income, expenses, mileage, invoices, and tax-ready exports.

## The Problem

- Independent performers operate like small businesses, but most software serving them is either too generic or spread across multiple disconnected tools.
- Their work is irregular, mobile, and multi-payer, which makes recordkeeping harder than it is for salaried workers or traditional small businesses.
- As a result, users often re-enter the same data multiple times, miss deductions, delay invoices, and reach tax season without a clean financial picture.

## The Solution

Bozzy is built around the actual operating flow of a working musician or solo freelancer:

- Track gigs, payouts, and payment status
- Capture expenses and store receipts
- Log mileage and estimate deduction value
- Manage payers, subcontractors, and 1099-related records
- Create and send invoices
- Export organized, tax-ready data for accountants and filing software

The core product advantage is simple: users capture information once, and Bozzy reuses it across profitability tracking, invoicing, compliance workflows, and year-end tax prep.

## Why The Product Can Win

- Vertical focus: Bozzy is designed for gig-based earners, not generic small-business accounting users.
- Strong workflow fit: gigs, tours, mileage, recurring expenses, invoice tracking, and tax exports live in one system instead of separate apps.
- Cross-platform reach: the product is built for web, iOS, and Android from a shared codebase.
- Automation: OCR-assisted receipt processing, billing sync, invoice status automation, and export validation reduce manual admin.
- Trust and security: user-scoped data access, MFA support, audit/security logging, and hardened auth flows are important when handling financial and tax records.

## Business Model

Bozzy is structured as a subscription software business with a free tier and paid upgrades. Premium access can expand usage limits and unlock higher-value workflows, while Stripe-based billing supports recurring revenue and plan enforcement.

## Why This Matters

Bozzy addresses a persistent pain point for a large, fragmented class of self-employed workers: people who earn income like a business but still manage their operations with consumer-grade tools. The wedge is musicians and gig-based performers, where workflow complexity is high and existing software fit is weak. From there, the same platform can extend into adjacent freelancer segments that share the same operational problems.

## Current Product Status

Bozzy already includes the core systems needed to support this category: income tracking, expense capture, mileage, invoicing, tax-ready exports, subscriptions, and security controls. The product architecture is already set up to support continued expansion in automation, compliance tooling, and premium monetization.
